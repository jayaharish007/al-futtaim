Our services

What we do offer

As a leader in our industry, we have always worked to develop advanced technology and innovative methods.

Engineering and Construction

We execute engineering services in a largely professional manner in association with in- house engineering division and with Approved & Internationally reputed standalone engineering companies according to nature & discipline of systems & scope of work.

Civil Construction

Due to the long span of experience Al Futtaim Group has got vast exposure of executing all types of Commercial and Industrial Projects as per international standards and project specifications.

Mechanical Construction

Al Futtaim Group has executed many Mechanical works such as
Fabrication, Aluminium, Fabrication, Steel Structure, Ducts & Chimneys, Blasting and Painting at shop & site, HVAC System, Fire Fighting System, Plumbing

Electrical & Instrumentation Construction

Al Futtaim Group can execute multiple E&I projects with various works structures such as;
* Power & Lighting (Building and Process Utilities)
* Power & Control Panel Installations (Switchgears, Switchboards, LP & MCC)
* LV, MV & HV Underground Cabling

Industrial, Commercial & Residential Buildings

Al Futtaim Group has got vast experience of executing all types of Industrial, Commercial and Residential Buildings such as Buildings and Power substation Buildings, Central Control Buildings, Pump Stations, Residential Apartments, Hotels & Villas etc.

Irrigation Projects

Our Technical Team is capable of executing all type of Irrigation projects such as Dams, Canals & Bridges including Survey, Engineering and Construction etc.

Marine Projects

Al Futtaim Group is a qualified contractor for executing Marine projects such as Construction of marine plat form, water Control sheet piling works, dredging & slopping of lakes, rivers, harbors, and other water bodies, shore protection works & marine excavations etc.

Operation & Maintenance Services

Al Futtaim Operation & Maintainance Division Provides Plant Operation, Plant Maintenance & Services. We have vast technical experience of executing Plant Shutdown and Plant Turnaround Services.

Security & Fire Fighting System Services

Al Futtaim Group also provides Complete Security & Fire Fighting System Services for the industrial and commercial requirements.

Rental of Skilled Manpower & Equipment

Al Futtaim Group also provides rental of equipments like Cranes Super Sucker, Boom Trucks, Side Boom, Wheel Loaders, Bull Dozers, Roller Compactor

Special Skilled Manpower
